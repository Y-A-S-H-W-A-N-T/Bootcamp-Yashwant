Amazon vs Flipkart: A Comparison

Amazon

Visual Style: Minimalistic and functional, with a white background and blue, orange, and black accents.

Navigation: Focused on usability with a prominent search bar, quick-access menus, and category-focused navigation.

Information Structure: Highly hierarchical and extensive, featuring detailed filters, personalized recommendations, and a multi-layered menu.

Strengths: Global appeal, accessibility, and optimized user experience for a wide audience.

Flipkart

Visual Style: Bold and vibrant, with a blue-dominant theme and yellow accents, reflecting an Indian audience's preferences.

Navigation: Compact and menu-driven, featuring dropdowns for categories and quick links for sales and offers.

Information Structure: Simplified yet comprehensive, with an emphasis on deals, trending products, and localized promotions.

Strengths: Regional focus, user-friendly mobile experience, and easy access to deals and offers.
